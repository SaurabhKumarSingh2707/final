Crop Planning

Plan your agricultural activities throughout the year for optimal yields

Kharif Season (June - October)

Season Overview

Kharif crops are sown at the beginning of the rainy season (June-July) and harvested at the end of monsoon (September-October). These crops require warm weather for germination and growth, and a rainy season for their development.

Rice
Sowing: June-July
Harvesting: October-November
Duration: 120-150 days
Water: High requirement (1500-2000mm)
Soil: Clay loam, well-drained
Cotton
Sowing: May-June
Harvesting: October-January
Duration: 180-200 days
Water: Moderate (600-1200mm)
Soil: Well-drained black cotton soil
Sugarcane
Sowing: February-April & October
Harvesting: 12-18 months
Duration: 300-365 days
Water: High requirement (2000-2500mm)
Soil: Deep, fertile, well-drained
Maize
Sowing: June-July
Harvesting: September-October
Duration: 80-120 days
Water: Moderate (500-800mm)
Soil: Well-drained loamy soil

Rabi Season (November - April)

Season Overview

Rabi crops are sown in winter (November-December) and harvested in spring (March-May). These crops require cool weather for germination and warm weather for growth and maturation.

Wheat
Sowing: November-December
Harvesting: March-April
Duration: 120-150 days
Water: Moderate (400-650mm)
Soil: Well-drained loamy soil
Barley
Sowing: October-November
Harvesting: March-April
Duration: 120-140 days
Water: Low-moderate (300-500mm)
Soil: Well-drained sandy loam
Mustard
Sowing: October-November
Harvesting: February-March
Duration: 90-120 days
Water: Low-moderate (300-400mm)
Soil: Well-drained sandy loam
Potato
Sowing: October-November
Harvesting: January-February
Duration: 90-120 days
Water: Moderate (500-700mm)
Soil: Well-drained sandy loam

Zaid Season (March - June)

Season Overview

Zaid crops are grown during the dry season with irrigation. These crops require hot weather and are typically grown in areas with assured irrigation facilities.

Watermelon
Sowing: March-April
Harvesting: May-June
Duration: 70-90 days
Water: High requirement (irrigation needed)
Soil: Well-drained sandy loam
Cucumber
Sowing: March-April
Harvesting: May-June
Duration: 60-80 days
Water: High requirement (irrigation needed)
Soil: Well-drained fertile loam
Fodder Crops
Sowing: March-April
Harvesting: May-June
Duration: 60-90 days
Water: Moderate-high (irrigation)
Soil: Various soil types
Summer Rice
Sowing: March-April
Harvesting: June-July
Duration: 90-120 days
Water: Very high (assured irrigation)
Soil: Clay loam with good water retention
