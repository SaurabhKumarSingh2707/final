How to Use

  1. Upload Image: Select or drag-and-drop a clear image of the affected plant part
  2. Configure Options: Enable TTA for better accuracy, choose number of predictions
  3. Analyze: Click "Analyze Plant" to start the AI detection process
  4. Review Results: Get detailed predictions with confidence scores and treatment advice
  5. Take Action: Follow the recommended treatment and prevention strategies

Important Notes

  • Use clear, well-lit images for best results
  • Focus on the affected area of the plant
  • Supported formats: PNG, JPG, JPEG, GIF, BMP, TIFF, WebP
  • Maximum file size: 32MB per image
  • Always consult with agricultural experts for serious cases
